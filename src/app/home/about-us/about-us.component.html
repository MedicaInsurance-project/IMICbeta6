Common Health Insurance Terms You Should Know

  • Proposer or Buyer: Also known as the policy owner, purchases the health insurance and pays for the premium.
  • Day care Procedures: 24-hour short-term hospitalization procedures that are also covered by health insurance.
  • Daily Cash: A health insurance feature that provides a fixed amount for every day during hospitalization.
  • Dependents: Your family members like children, spouse, parents or any other relatives who benefit from the insurance purchased.

What Is Health Insurance?

Medical costs are rising at an alarming rate. Health insurance safeguards you against the increasing expenses, by reimbursing you or directly paying the medical fees, and also offers a host of other benefits such as free check-ups and ambulance services. The earlier you get insured in life, the lesser would be your premium.
